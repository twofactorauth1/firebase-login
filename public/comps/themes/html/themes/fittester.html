About

Ken Nicodemus, MA.

Exercise Physiologist & coach

Ken is the director and founder of The Fit Stop Human Performance Lab in Carlsbad, CA. His work involves developing and implementing a variety of sophisticated assessment and guidance programs to improve the health and physical performance levels of endurance athletes, employees and the general population. He has delivered and managed wellness screening programs for a number of prestigious clients including the California Highway Patrol, Calif. Department of Corrections, the City of Santa Monica, PacifiCare Health Systems, the Los Angeles Clippers, Sempra Utilities, Navy Special Warfare, along with numerous fire and police departments throughout California. Early in his career I worked as an exercise physiologist in the clinical arena including physical therapy and cardiac rehabilitation.

“Working with clients and helping them train to reach their physical best is extremely rewarding. Examining the heights one can reach or trekking around impasses is a challenge I look forward to.”

- Ken
