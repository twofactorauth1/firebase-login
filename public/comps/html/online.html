Okay, it's time to start marketing your website online. If you need assitance use any of the three ways below.

Watch this 30 second video about marketing online.

Schedule a Demo

Schedule a personal demo with one of our customer support representatives who can show you via screen share how to market online.

Read the Documentation

If you prefer a list of steps to read, then head over to our detailed documentation about all the aspects of marketing online.

Have someone write an article.

Customers read articles all the time, but who has time to write an article and make it interesting for people to read. To get a article written for you all you need to do is come up with the subject and a couple sentences describing what you want the article to be about. That's it! Once approved the article can be posted on your blog or be sent in a press release.

Send out a Press Release

Putting your blog posts on your blog isn't enough if you don't have the traffic. Send your best articles and events through press releases to get your content on many sites to maximize visibility. The press release will link back to your site thus giving an addtional avenue for customers to find your business.

Start Google ADs

If you have a budget for marketing and you want instant traffic to your site then Google Ads is a perfect match. We will assist you in choosing the keywords you need to target in order to maximize your budget and get customers who will buy.
